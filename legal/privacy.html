What information may be collected?

ZCraft Studios may collect information you submit through forms or direct contact, including Discord username, email address, project type, target platform, budget, timeline, references, request details, support messages, and donation-related confirmation details.

How is the information used?

Information is used to respond to messages, prepare quotes, deliver custom work, provide support, improve resources, prevent abuse, maintain records, and keep the website and services reliable.

Which third-party services may be involved?

The site may link to or use services such as Discord, PayPal, GitHub, BuiltByBit, image hosting, Vercel, browser fonts, and other infrastructure providers. Those services may process data according to their own policies.

Are payments stored by ZCraft Studios?

Payment processing is handled by third-party payment providers such as PayPal. ZCraft Studios does not need to store full payment card details on this website.

How long is data kept?

Project and contact records may be kept as long as needed for support, accounting, dispute handling, security, and operational history. You can contact ZCraft Studios to ask about correction or deletion where practical and legally allowed.

How can privacy questions be sent?

Privacy questions can be sent through the contact page or by email at zain@z-craft.xyz. Include enough context to identify the request without sending unnecessary sensitive information.
