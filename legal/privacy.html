Privacy Policy

What information may be collected?

ZCraft Studios may collect information you submit through forms or direct contact, including your name or display name, Discord username, email address, project type, target platform, server version, plugin list, dashboard requirements, website requirements, budget, timeline, references, request details, support messages, and donation-related confirmation details.

How is the information used?

Information is used to respond to messages, prepare quotes, understand project requirements, deliver custom work, provide support, improve resources, prevent abuse, maintain records, protect the website, and keep services reliable.

What Minecraft or server-related data may be shared?

Clients may choose to share Minecraft server details such as server versions, plugin lists, configuration files, screenshots, logs, dashboard requirements, player-facing feature examples, or branding assets. ZCraft Studios uses this information only to understand, build, fix, or support the requested work.

What Discord-related data may be involved?

Discord bot projects may involve Discord usernames, server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, bot permissions, ticket data, application data, logs, or command usage depending on the requested features. Clients should only provide access and permissions that are necessary for the project.

Which third-party services may be involved?

The site may link to or use services such as Discord, PayPal, GitHub, BuiltByBit, Vercel, databases, image hosting, browser fonts, analytics tools, and other infrastructure providers. Those services may process data according to their own policies.

Are payments stored by ZCraft Studios?

Payment processing is handled by third-party payment providers such as PayPal or marketplace platforms. ZCraft Studios does not need to store full payment card details on this website.

How long is data kept?

Project and contact records may be kept as long as needed for support, accounting, dispute handling, security, abuse prevention, and operational history. You can contact ZCraft Studios to ask about correction or deletion where practical and legally allowed.

How is information protected?

ZCraft Studios uses reasonable steps to protect project information and avoid unnecessary access or sharing. However, no website, email system, hosting provider, Discord server, payment provider, or online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

How can privacy questions be sent?

Privacy questions can be sent through the contact page or by email at zain@z-craft.xyz. Include enough context to identify the request without sending unnecessary sensitive information.
