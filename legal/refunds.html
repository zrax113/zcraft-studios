Refund and Cancellation Policy

This policy explains how refunds, cancellations, revisions, donations, support, and digital product issues are handled for ZCraft Studios resources and custom work.

Effective date 2026-06-15Last updated 2026-06-15ZCraft Studios

How are downloadable resources handled?

Downloadable digital resources may have limited refund eligibility once files, links, licenses, or access have been delivered. If a resource has a technical issue, contact ZCraft Studios with the product name, platform, version, screenshots, logs, and setup details.

How are custom project cancellations handled?

Custom project cancellation depends on project stage. Work that has already been planned, designed, coded, configured, tested, delivered, or reserved in the schedule may not be fully refundable. Any refund is reviewed against completed work and agreed scope.

What about revisions?

Reasonable fixes for confirmed bugs or agreed deliverables are handled through the support method listed for the product or project. New features, changed requirements, platform changes, or extra integrations may require a new quote.

Are donations refundable?

Donations are voluntary support for the studio and are generally not treated as purchases of a specific product or deliverable. If a donation was accidental, contact ZCraft Studios quickly with the payment details and context.

What information is needed for a refund review?

Include your order or payment reference, product or project name, contact method, issue description, screenshots, logs, platform versions, and steps to reproduce the problem.
